FRONT LEVER GUIDE

Progress toward the front lever for elite back and core strength.

Proper Form

  • Hang from bar with shoulder engagement.
  • Retract scapulae and brace entire body.
  • Raise body horizontal with arms straight.
  • Keep body in one rigid line.
  • Hold or lower through tuck/straddle progressions.

Common Mistakes

  • Bent arms during hold.
  • Piking hips instead of straight body.
  • Loose scapular engagement.
  • Progressing too fast without basics.
  • Insufficient pull-up and row strength.

Benefits

  • Elite lat and core strength builder.
  • Develops straight-arm pulling power.
  • Impressive static strength skill.
  • Improves scapular control.
  • Goal movement for advanced athletes.
