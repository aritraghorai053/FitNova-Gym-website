HANDSTAND PUSH-UPS GUIDE

Develop vertical pressing strength and balance with handstand push-up progressions.

Proper Form

  • Kick up to wall-supported handstand.
  • Lower head toward floor with control.
  • Keep core tight and body in one line.
  • Press back to full arm extension.
  • Use pike or box progressions if needed.

Common Mistakes

  • Arching back excessively away from wall.
  • Incomplete range of motion.
  • Hands too wide or narrow.
  • Bailing without safe exit plan.
  • Attempting without shoulder strength base.

Benefits

  • Builds shoulder and tricep strength.
  • Improves balance and body control.
  • Develops overhead pressing power.
  • Impressive skill-based progression.
  • Strengthens core and wrists.
