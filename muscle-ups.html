MUSCLE-UPS GUIDE

Combine pulling and pushing into one of calisthenics' most impressive skills.

Proper Form

  • Start with false grip or standard pull-up grip.
  • Pull explosively chest to bar height.
  • Transition wrists over bar quickly.
  • Press out to support position on top.
  • Lower with control through each phase.

Common Mistakes

  • Insufficient pull height before transition.
  • Kipping wildly without control.
  • Slow transition causing failed reps.
  • Not practicing negatives separately.
  • Skipping prerequisite pull-up strength.

Benefits

  • Ultimate upper-body strength display.
  • Combines pull and push in one move.
  • Builds bar skills and confidence.
  • Improves explosive pulling power.
  • Gateway to advanced bar work.
