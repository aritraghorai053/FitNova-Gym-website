WALL BALLS GUIDE

Combine squat power and throwing accuracy for intense CrossFit conditioning.

Proper Form

  • Hold med ball at chest in front rack.
  • Squat to depth keeping chest up.
  • Drive up and throw ball to target.
  • Use leg power to assist the throw.
  • Catch ball and flow into next rep.

Common Mistakes

  • Throwing only with arms, not legs.
  • Dropping chest during squat.
  • Throwing off-target consistently.
  • Catching ball with loose core.
  • Using ball weight too heavy.

Benefits

  • Full-body cardio and strength combo.
  • Improves squat endurance under load.
  • Builds shoulder and core power.
  • Great for high-rep conditioning.
  • Develops hand-eye coordination.
