HEAVY BAG TRAINING GUIDE

Develop striking power, endurance, and fight-ready conditioning on the heavy bag.

Proper Form

  • Stay on balls of feet in fighting stance.
  • Rotate hips and shoulders into each strike.
  • Keep hands up for defense between combos.
  • Exhale sharply on each punch or kick.
  • Move around the bag, don't stand static.

Common Mistakes

  • Arm-only punching without hip rotation.
  • Dropping guard after combinations.
  • Flat-footed stance reducing mobility.
  • Holding breath during intense rounds.
  • Over-swinging and losing balance.

Benefits

  • Builds punching power and speed.
  • Improves cardiovascular endurance.
  • Develops coordination and timing.
  • Releases stress and builds confidence.
  • Simulates real fight conditioning.
