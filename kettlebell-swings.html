KETTLEBELL SWINGS GUIDE

Generate hip power and conditioning with efficient kettlebell swing mechanics.

Proper Form

  • Hinge at hips with flat back.
  • Snap hips forward to propel the bell.
  • Keep arms relaxed, bell floats to chest height.
  • Brace core at the top of each rep.
  • Let bell swing back between legs under control.

Common Mistakes

  • Squatting instead of hinging.
  • Lifting with arms instead of hips.
  • Overextending lower back at top.
  • Bell drifting too far from body.
  • Using weight too heavy for form.

Benefits

  • Develops explosive hip power.
  • Improves posterior chain strength.
  • Burns calories efficiently.
  • Builds grip and core endurance.
  • Low-impact cardio option.
