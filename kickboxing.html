KICKBOXING GUIDE

Combine punches and kicks for explosive MMA-style fitness and agility.

Proper Form

  • Maintain balanced fighting stance.
  • Snap kicks with hip rotation, not pushing.
  • Return kicks quickly to guard position.
  • Flow punches into kicks in combinations.
  • Keep chin tucked and core engaged.

Common Mistakes

  • Overcommitting on kicks and falling off balance.
  • Dropping hands when kicking.
  • Telegraphing strikes before throwing.
  • Stiff, robotic movement patterns.
  • Ignoring footwork between strikes.

Benefits

  • Full-body cardio and power training.
  • Improves flexibility and hip mobility.
  • Builds speed and reaction time.
  • Enhances coordination and balance.
  • Fun, engaging fat-burning workout.
