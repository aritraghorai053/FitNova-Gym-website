THRUSTERS GUIDE

Combine front squats and overhead presses for explosive CrossFit performance.

Proper Form

  • Hold bar in front rack position.
  • Squat to depth with upright torso.
  • Drive explosively out of the squat.
  • Use leg momentum to press bar overhead.
  • Lock out and return bar to front rack.

Common Mistakes

  • Pressing too early before hips extend.
  • Losing front rack position.
  • Shallow squat depth.
  • Pressing bar forward off-line.
  • Dropping elbows during the squat.

Benefits

  • Full-body power and conditioning.
  • Builds leg drive and overhead strength.
  • High calorie burn in short time.
  • Improves cardiovascular endurance.
  • Core CrossFit competition movement.
