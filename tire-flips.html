TIRE FLIPS GUIDE

Flip your way to raw power with proper tire flip technique and bracing.

Proper Form

  • Squat down with wide stance at tire base.
  • Drive with legs, lift tire to knee height.
  • Transition hands to push tire forward-up.
  • Keep back flat during initial pull.
  • Walk forward as tire comes over.

Common Mistakes

  • Rounding back during the initial lift.
  • Biceps-dominant curling of the tire.
  • Using a tire too heavy for technique.
  • Feet too narrow for stable base.
  • Rushing without resetting grip.

Benefits

  • Builds total-body explosive strength.
  • Develops grip and leg drive.
  • Improves functional power output.
  • Great for MMA strength circuits.
  • Builds mental toughness under load.
