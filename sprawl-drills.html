SPRAWL DRILLS GUIDE

Train the sprawl for explosive defense, core strength, and MMA conditioning.

Proper Form

  • Start in athletic stance, hands up.
  • Shoot hips back quickly as legs kick back.
  • Land in strong plank with hips down.
  • Immediately pop back to standing.
  • Repeat with controlled explosive tempo.

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping hips too low into a push-up.
  • Landing with sagging core in plank.
  • Slow, lazy hip movement.
  • Feet crossing during the kick-back.
  • Not returning to proper stance.

Benefits

  • Builds explosive hip power.
  • Improves defensive grappling reflexes.
  • High-intensity full-body conditioning.
  • Strengthens core and shoulders.
  • Develops fight-specific endurance.
