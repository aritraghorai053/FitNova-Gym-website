ROWING GUIDE

Maximize cardio output and full-body engagement on the rowing machine.

Proper Form

  • Drive with legs first, then lean back slightly.
  • Pull handle to lower ribs, not neck.
  • Return in reverse order: arms, body, legs.
  • Maintain steady stroke rate and rhythm.
  • Keep shoulders relaxed and core engaged.

Common Mistakes

  • Pulling with arms before leg drive.
  • Rounding upper back at the catch.
  • Jerky, inconsistent stroke timing.
  • Over-reaching at the front.
  • Gripping handle too tightly.

Benefits

  • Low-impact full-body cardio.
  • Builds back, legs, and arm endurance.
  • Improves cardiovascular capacity.
  • Burns high calories efficiently.
  • Ideal for CrossFit WOD finishers.
