MEDICINE BALL SLAMS GUIDE

Unleash explosive core and upper-body power with medicine ball slams.

Proper Form

  • Lift ball overhead with extended body.
  • Slam ball down using full core and lats.
  • Follow through with hips and arms.
  • Catch or pick up ball for next rep.
  • Maintain athletic stance throughout.

Common Mistakes

  • Rounding back when lifting ball up.
  • Using only arms without core snap.
  • Standing too far from bounce zone.
  • Ball weight too heavy for speed.
  • Poor breathing rhythm during sets.

Benefits

  • Develops explosive core power.
  • Builds lat and shoulder strength.
  • High-intensity calorie burning.
  • Releases tension with powerful movement.
  • Improves athletic power output.
