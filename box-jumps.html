BOX JUMPS GUIDE

Build explosive leg power and athletic coordination with safe box jump technique.

Proper Form

  • Stand arm's length from the box.
  • Swing arms and dip slightly before jumping.
  • Land softly with full foot on box.
  • Stand tall to complete the rep.
  • Step down carefully — avoid rebounding down.

Common Mistakes

  • Landing with stiff, straight legs.
  • Feet clipping the edge of the box.
  • Jumping down repeatedly (high injury risk).
  • Using a box too high for skill level.
  • Poor arm timing reducing height.

Benefits

  • Develops explosive lower-body power.
  • Improves agility and coordination.
  • Builds fast-twitch muscle fibers.
  • Enhances athletic performance.
  • Scalable for all fitness levels.
